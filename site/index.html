Cloud Seeding 101

Cloud seeding injects silver iodide or salt into clouds to trigger rainfall. Potential benefits include particulate wash-out (reducing PM2.5) and drought relief, but critics highlight unknown long-term ecological side-effects.

  • ✔ Can lower dust and smoke concentrations locally for 24-48 h
  • ✖ May shift pollution downwind, not remove it globally
  • ✖ Silver accumulation in soil/water is still under study

Data snapshot: WHO & OpenAQ, Jan 1 – Dec 31 2024.
